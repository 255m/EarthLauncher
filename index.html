What devices are supported? ما هي الأجهزة المدعومة؟

EarthLauncher supports Android devices with version 7.0 or higher. Devices with at least 2GB of RAM are recommended for optimal performance.

يدعم EarthLauncher أجهزة الأندرويد بالإصدار 7.0 أو أعلى. يوصى بأجهزة بها 2GB RAM على الأقل لأداء مثالي.

Is EarthLauncher free to use? هل EarthLauncher مجاني؟

Yes, EarthLauncher is completely free to download and use. We may offer premium features in the future, but the core functionality will always remain free.
